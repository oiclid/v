Rise Against Peer Pressure #Rapp (Mixtape).

It's good and intelligent music or nothing!
After much anticipation from lovers of good music, this prolific Kroc-city rapper #D.I.A shows again that he is always capable of putting out classic pieces of art in his third effort. Rise above peer pressure #RAPP is what he calls this one. A must listen for every Nigerian youth out there as it entails songs on a lot of societal vices that the average Nigerian youth find hard to resist on daily basis. Production credits go to D.I.A's alter-ego CandyMan, Zayne-F, and DJ Notte among others. With no major features besides a young rap scholar/protégée in person of J.O.B. & of cos...... D.I.A :) The entire project was mixed & mastered by #Metro. It is time for good and intelligent music #GAIM.

365 days Later (EP)

Exactly 365 days after releasing his debut mixtape, Zaria born rapper D.I.A is back with an extended play and he calls it "365 days later ep". It is solely a compilation of songs he made after dropping his first mixtape and dedicates to lovers of his music. Y'all should enjoy this collection of tunes that couldn't make it into the TMI mixtape.

Endeavour to download, share & support this rising king.

Too Much Intellisense (Mixtape)

The idea of Nigerian hip-hop being dead should automatically evaporate from your head if you successfully download and listen to this rare masterpiece from this prolific KD based rapper popularly known as D.I.A (dual intelligence artillery). Too much Intellisense (TMI) is what he calls his debut effort, embedded with dope/positive contents, rhythm, creativity and versatility (good and intelligent music).GAIM Is the right phrase to describe this piece.

Collaborating with the likes of (Kheengz, Gotheeq, Izrayl Tha Griot, Snowflake, RANE, Ross of BHP, Skimmo, Raamoney, MoneyBagg and a lot of others) + special credits to producers (zayneF, Acebeatz, Ultra, Cza Tha Griot, SARZ and Da CandyMan (D.I.A himself :). It might surprise you to know that he' s just in his early 20' s. Although a lot have come and gone but will this young and dope rapper stand the test of time?,.... only time can tell.
