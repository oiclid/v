Born Didam Iliya Ajeye, D.I.A also stands for "Didam is Amazing", "Dual Intelligence Artillery", "Damn Iâ€™m Awesome", "Dan Iskan Aboki", etc.

Born and raised in Zaria, Kaduna State on March 28th, 1991, D.I.A comes from Zangon-Kataf. As befits a rapper who has been writing and perfoming for over 10 years, D.I.A has been described as having a maturity and style that belies his relatively young age, and has been compared to the Likes of top notch contemporary international rap act in a list comprising J-cole, Kendrick Lamar, Casper Nyovest, as well as Nigerian rap veterans M.I Abaga and Jesse Jagz.

D.I.A has a degree in Metalurgical and Materials Engineering from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria. He is also a veteran of several rap battles, winning several, including Nokia ™ Rapp Battle A.B.U Edition (2012); and Made in Croc City #ShutItDown Rap Battle (2014). He was also won the Made in Croc City Song of the Year Award in 2013. He is thus poised to join a long line of fine Emcees who were raised in Kaduna

D.I.A's influences include Nasir Jones, Kanye West, Master Ace, and Nate Dogg, among others. He happens to have an alter-ego (who is a master producer) called "CandyMan". While he is currently not signed to any record label, he is one of the fore-runners of Good And Intelligent Music (GAIM), as well as being the founder of the Arewa Boys Conglomerate (ABC).
